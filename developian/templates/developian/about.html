Developian is a self-development brand which encourages big dreams, setting visions, and working aggresively towards making them a reality.



Developian's power lies in the community. We have an ambitious culture which strives for greatness. We really shoot for the moon. While most people find comfort in the simple lifestyle, we thrive in discomfort.


Every single day is another opportunity. One to conquer, or to be conquered. Through regular goal setting, and daily scheduling, we hold ourselves accountable to working towards our vision, and conquering this life.


About the creator:

Have you ever found scheduling and goal setting apps hard to stick to? Well I definetely have. If you are a highly driven individual who wants to acheive great things, than we may be similar. My name is Michal Buczek and I created this brand to help people like you, (and even myself), develop a system of accountability and planning to help organize our busy lives and aspirations. We all know that we should be more organized with how we spend our time, but little of us actually do it. This website aims to solve that problem. So if your new here, give it a shot. It might stick with you or it might not, but it's important that you find out for yourself. Only those who take bold opporunities, have the potential to live a bold life. ~ Michal Buczek
